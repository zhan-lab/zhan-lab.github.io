Physics-based AI on Robots

Building upon the HTPVD (High Throughput Solution-Processable Photovoltaic Materials Discovery Facility) system at Monash University, this project will deploy our physics-base AI analysis on a robotic platform located at Monash University.

Funding

This research is based on a collaborative ACAP2 project among Monash, ANU, and UNSW entitled Bayesian Optimization and Large Language Modules: Towards Autonomous, Closed-Loop PV Materials Discovery.
